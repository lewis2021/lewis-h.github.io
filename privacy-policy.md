
# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Moms Brain Games is developed and provided by NEXALAB.

This Privacy Policy explains how Moms Brain Games handles user information.

## Information We Collect

Moms Brain Games does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or messages.

The app may store gameplay information locally on the user's device, such as:

- Game progress
- Scores
- Best records
- Settings such as language, timer, and sound preferences

This information is stored only on the device and is not sent to our server.

## Advertising

Moms Brain Games may display advertisements through third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

Advertising services may collect or use device information, advertising identifiers, app activity, or approximate information in order to provide, limit, or measure ads according to their own policies.

For more information, please refer to Google's policies:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children

Moms Brain Games is designed as a casual brain game that can be enjoyed by general users, including younger users.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We do not share personal information with third parties except where required by law or where third-party services such as advertising providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay records and settings stored locally on the device remain until the user deletes the app data or uninstalls the app.

Users can delete locally stored data by clearing the app data in the device settings or uninstalling the app.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
